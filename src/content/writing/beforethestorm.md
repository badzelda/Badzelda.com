---<div class=bodytext>Electric Mary mainlining it<br> 
on New York's veins, glows<br> 
at the wall, naked brickwork stained<br> 
with life, years, age<br> 
in this new place, fast and always<br> 
creating itself.<br> 
<br> 
We are the lunatics, stalking<br> 
sane people hiding in Bellevue from<br> 
the seething streets, gridlocked, and<br> 
screaming horns venting frustration<br> 
walk, don't walk.<br> 
<br> 
Central station silences all, the quiet<br> 
dome of the heavens overlooks all,<br> 
ordains all with its pinprick zodiac,<br> 
a cavern in the belly of a skyscraper.<br> 
<br> 
Houses perch at the tops of buildings,<br> 
stairways snake along their sides<br> 
trees of heaven sprout from unlikely ledges <br> 
fighting the gargoyles perched,<br> 
and quietly guard all.<br></div>
