Looking at the world <br> 
through my hole in the ice<br> 
I see the word ritual<br> 
scrawled on the door of a church.<br><br> 
 
Is this that purely human ritual<br> 
of wake eat sleep converse<br> 
work earn die?<br><br> 
 
That age long empty practice<br> 
called Natural Law - how<br><br> 
 
natural, that artificial<br> 
subcultural self-created reification<br> 
of dominant rule.<br><br> 
 
My sun shines down upon me,<br> 
upon the melting ice.<br> 
Layer upon layer <br> 
palimpsest reality emerges.<br><br>
