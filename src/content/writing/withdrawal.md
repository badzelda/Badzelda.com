---<div class=bodytext>Once upon a time there wod a nebbly nod<br>
Wot I trothe, ee knew im 'ow to plod;<br>
'Ee plod 'is trothe day be wibbly day,<br>
Eek non wot knew 'im listened wot ee say.<br>
Ee say "Jimbly lost and flying high<br>
Wid colours bright im psychadelic pie"<br>
and world is cold, shivers setting in<br>
he sees not hears not pain as liken to a pin<br>
And his is not to reason why<br>
'is fearly clash is foughten day by bleeding day;<br>
'Ee struggles on, and Eek as well ee may.<br>
