Once....<br> 
life was less complicated<br> 
then, the multifarious<br> 
involuntary multitude<br> 
<br> 
(man, still subject to decay)<br> 
<br> 
imposed their imperfection<br> 
like some base concrete annex<br> 
on the city's tall towers<br> 
<br> 
(she<br> 
 is caught in progression<br> 
 the disease of time)<br> 
<br> 
the form obscene<br> 
creeps, flies, runs, crawls:<br> 
grows.<br> 
<br> 
("and this is nature's nest of boxes)<br> 
<br> 
Copernicus kills God<br> 
and we are left feeble<br> 
hands holding to a pale shadow<br> 
gone askew<br> 
<br> 
(the one within another <br> 
 and  so on<br> 
 to eternity)<br> 
<br> 
information replaces <br> 
belief - structures grow <br> 
and myth is forfeit<br> 
In this age of 'information'<br> 
where is certainty ?<br> 
<br> 
(and the common centre to all is <br> 
 decay; decay and ruin".)<br> 
<br><br> 
10/3/96<br>
