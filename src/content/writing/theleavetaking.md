---<div class=bodytext>This farewell is a silent one:<br>
Inscribed on your skin<br>
With lips and fingers;<br>
Implicit in the twinings <br>
Of hip and breast and thigh.<br>
Don't expect words from me<br>
Or tearful partings.<br><br>

And please don't tell me that you love me<br>
Whispering caresses in my ear<br>
With Judas kisses that leave me cold.<br>
Words fly the air like decieving imps<br>
Or stagger in painful and protracted march<br>
Between us.<br>
Farewell my foolish lover - <br>
Let only your touch<br>
Wipe your nimble fingers from my flesh,<br>
And shared breath brush thoughts of you<br>
From my head.<br><br>
1990
