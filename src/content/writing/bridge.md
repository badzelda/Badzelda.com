---<div class=bodytext>Girders shiver in massive convocation<BR> - a backward stretching harmony in unlikely elevation<BR> while to seaward and landward the taut strung cables,<BR> the seamist and rugged cloud remind me of fables<BR> that opposition is life; existence is strife;<BR> And all is divided on the blade of a knife.<BR> Bridging the gap with words, creating tension;<BR> stressing language's asymmetrical scansion<br><br>Sets against that difference the fragility<BR> Which cannot be hid save through lexical agility.<BR><BR>
1996
