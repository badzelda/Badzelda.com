<p>I've been writing since 1985 - poetry first, published in journals and sites including the Poetry Library, Panic! Brixton Poets Society, and UNESCO's 'Poetica Babele', and performed live at spoken word nights around London.</p>
<p>These days it's mostly novels. Find out more about them below.</p>
<p>I'm also the founder of <a href="http://www.brixtonbookjam.com" target="_new">Brixton BookJam</a> - a free quarterly literary night I've been running in Brixton for 13 years, featuring five-minute readings from established and emerging writers. There are books, but it's not bookish. There is no jam! If you'd like to take part, <a href="mailto:newsletter@brixtonbookjam.com">contact us</a>.</p>
<p>You'll also find some short stories and articles here, written for various publications.</p>
