And all these masks over here are the work of a single artist - as you can see he has a fondness for black and red:<br> 
<br> 
Here on the right is a famous work - 'Tamburlaine', where on the tips of bright spears<br> 
perch 100 furies - they are waiting for the signal.<br> 
<br> 
This one is said to be the artist's favourite - the faces are familiar, prototypes:<br> 
he's called it pseudomimesis - self-propagating people<br> 
<br> 
And the title of this exhibition? Surely to title it is to complete it,<br> 
And as the artist says: The universe is always dreaming itself into being.<br><br>
