Hand in hand beneath the sheets they revel<br>
in one another's rancid farts;<BR> 
the years passed, and neither<BR> 
much interested in the passing fancy.
<br><br>
Creaking bedsprings once a week<br>
in clockwork motion speak of habit:<br>
those and the sheets soft with much washing<br>
and the nightly restlessness of bodies.<br><br>
When the morning comes<BR> 
they will leave off such intimacy, tucking it<BR> mechanically beneath the sheets<BR>
of their lifetime-guaranteed nuptial bed.</div>
