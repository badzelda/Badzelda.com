---<div class=bodytext>The years have made him blind<br>
Both to his work and praise - <br>
Now he sculpts with his fingertips<br>
What others appreciate with their eyes.<br><br>

Whilst they are marvelling at symmetries<br>
Of form, and the artist's self deprecating irony<br>
He is washing down stonedust with a pint<br>
In his local - they know his habits.<br><br>

And while critics strain to fathom<br>
His inner vision, adding words upon words <br>
To those already written, he is filling out form P37<br>
For a new set of National Health glasses.<br><br>
1997
