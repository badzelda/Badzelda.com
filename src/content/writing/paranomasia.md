she is still counting time<br> 
time and again standing still<br> 
stock still taking stock still wondering<br> 
wandering without aim<br> 
 
aiming high yet still laid low<br> 
by the fear of change still changing<br> 
the fear of stasis where ecstasy<br> 
is joy in stillness<br><br> 
1996
