Melting, melting - only the solid<br>
Weight of a pen in my hand<br>
Keeps me anchored<br><br>

In your sea of lies - <br>
22 thousand feet up, and <br>
It's still the same:<br><br>

The void of me <br>
Draws you in <br>
On the cold tide<br><br>

Trained to slash the eyes<br>
You'd rather have a machete<br>
Than a carving knife.<br><br>

Can I trap you in aveil of hair:<br>
Smother you,<br>
Castrate you with the tangled<br><br>

Strands <br>
Of my desire?<br>
Kamikaze man - you kill me.<br><br>
