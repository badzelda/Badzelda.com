---<div class=bodytext>The sun shines face full and brave<br> 
upon this street where humanity crawls - time<br> 
stopped and the same man on the same corner<br> 
is still calculating the secret of the universe:<br> 
he has his own agenda.<br><br> 
 
Full of his own mystery, he sits swaying<br> 
moaning, mumbling Kabala into his beard,<br> 
brow wrinkled and his eyes lost.<br> 
Time he worked it out, whatever it is,<br> 
stopped staring through pedestrians, stopped sleeping there<br> 
and shouting wildly at cars from his<br> 
island of boxes.<br><br> 
 
His agents are everywhere, communing telepathically<br> 
with pigeons, graffiti artists, Greek restaurant owners<br> 
and the proprietors of cat houses in Soho;<br> 
keeping him abreast of the city's ciphers<br> 
add numbers to his columns.<br><br> 
 
The statistician of the stars, the holder of runes.<br> 
Changes the script, gets with the planet, removes <br> 
scales from his eyes: the aliens are landing.<br><br> 
1997
