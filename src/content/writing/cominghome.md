---<div class=bodytext>Maybe the moon is full, dogs<br>are howling in backyards; crisp<br>light etched on double glazing - stripped<br>all bare, all not holding falling<br>apart that claws<br>at the warmth fast vanishing<br>Lost in the surgical glint<br>of linoleum, the patterned <br>chintz of a plastic<br>shower curtain. </p><p>A shell of a house - that<br>was how she wanted it<br> - remade and better!<br>A little less optimistic this time perhaps - dove <br>grey laps like the tide over yesterday's yellow. </p><p>How will the lady hang upon the walls, and <br>that long-untuned piano stand <br> that lends the halls an air<br>of - is it vanished grace?</div>
