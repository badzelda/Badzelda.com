---<BLOCKQUOTE>(Circular movements of reaping in <BR> stubbled cornfields<BR> where the scream of the blue banded hawk<BR> pierces the sky, and)<BR></BLOCKQUOTE><P>From somewhere vast a voice<BR> Comes rumbling into my dreams of flight;<BR> And taut as the backward strung lute<BR> A body arches under me.<BLOCKQUOTE>	(gasping I lie on the shore, watching<BR> the speechless waves receding.<BR> The sand is damp and smooth beneath my naked body -<BR> between the sea and the sand is a time for thought.)<BR></BLOCKQUOTE>I have yet to write the book of that body:<BR> Rich in the promise of a change, the<BR> Grey ghost of a formless text, waiting<BR> For me to create it again.<BLOCKQUOTE>	(I lay down in the cornfield; small creatures<BR> explore my stillness,<BR> the banded hawk plummets<BR> to mark me with blue armlets of bruises.)</BLOCKQUOTE><p>I was falling dizzyingly <BR> Into an illusion<BR> You have broken - <BR> For that I am grateful.<BR><BR> 
1992</p>
