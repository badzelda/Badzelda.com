---<i>"O wild sweet festal odes,<br> 
frenzy without joy" (Caligula, Camus)</i><br><br> 
 
later<br> 
the sound of the waterclock<br> 
prerecorded birdsong and the grey dawn<br><br> 
 
like spent matches, or Dionysus' children<br> 
burnt out heads and twisted bodies<br> 
litter the carpet<br><br> 
 
someone is still chasing the dragon,<br> 
speed on the gums and sleep<br> 
perfoming an antic dance before their eyes<br><br> 
 
I smell sorrow, sweat, and the sweet tang of opium.<br> 
 
<br>1994
