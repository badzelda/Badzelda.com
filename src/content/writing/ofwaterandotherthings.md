for Rina <br><br> 
 
I dream a golden beach with sand <br> 
between my toes, blue sky and<br> 
the white rushing sea in my ears:<br> 
I am barefoot; my clothes ripple.<br><br> 
 
 
 
Dream people talk to me as <br> 
I walk, in French - I reply <br> 
in the same language speaking<br> 
throatily with polished vowels.<br><br> 
 
 
 
In my dream I cross train tracks<br> 
and hear shouting -a red steam<br> 
engine and the driver pointing at me - <br> 
suddenly the sea is loud in my ears<br><br> 
 
 
 
the station and the red train have vanished, <br> 
I am still following the line of the dream shore;<br> 
nearby is someone I loved, now gone<br> 
waiting for me to return<br><br> 
1990
