I cannot paint a picture in oils to capture its beauty;<BR> I cannot weave melodies to contain its music;<BR> I can only write:<BR> Of the leaves that swirl in complicated patterns, <BR> The trees that bend and sway in gusts<BR> The glowering sky and avenues of colour.<BR> Couples walk arm in arm,<BR> Wrapped in each other against the cold<BR> And flights of pigeons glide like autumn leaves<BR> Grey against grey sky<BR> To land in a body losing all grace in the action.<BR> These words, this pen, are lonely - <BR> Autumn is a cold time of year. 
<br><br> 
1993
