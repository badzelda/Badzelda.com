There's that old inconstant moon, <br> 
mistress of tides, and change, and flux.<br> 
Who cares for her now? <br><br> 
 
Hers is the suicides slot<br> 
when it seems everything's fled <br> 
from the world, then<br> 
the stealer of loves throws her monochrome<br> 
silence across the land.<br><br> 
 
Sure she's always playing that tart's game<br> 
of wax and wane;<br> 
to and fro with the tidal rip<br> 
Holding the wide sea's reins in her hands.<br><br>
