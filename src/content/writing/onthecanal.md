This travelling feels like drifting; only the engine's <br>
Muted roar to break the silence<br>
Of these springtime woods;<br>
Their unsuspected beauty caught<br>
Between underpass and city where<br>
Stark budding branches arch<br>
to the azure sky.<br>
<br>
We steer by numbered bridges, distance<br>
Measured only by the passing hours, prow whispering<br>
Through the still water from dawn to dusk:<br>
The rising banks conceal us from the giddy world. <br>
Shore bound fishermen raise sleepy navelgazes as we pass.<br>
<br>
Now shadowed by the falling dusk, we moor:<br>
shutting warm wooded panels studded with painted flowers,<br>
chimneys lazily spilling smoke downriver whilst we<br>
Drift sleepwards to the barge's gentle rocking, <br>
Held outside time and tide.<br>
