There's that glint in your eye,<br>
The wicked grin, the wild<br>
Gesticulations<br>
Articulations, perambulations<br>
Through topics arcane and obscure:<br>
The unfathomable compels you.<br><br>

Still memory paints you with such tender tones,<br>
Mellows the light in your eye,<br>
Targets the fleeting restful moment;<br>
The hollowed arm, the nested cheek,<br>
The body lulled to calm <br>
By sleep . . . I will not disturb it.<br><br>

But cast my thoughts in a net of dreams<br>
Where nothing is but only seems.<br>
I'll go there, looking here, and through distance <br>
Some perspective gain - begin to train<br>
The sprinting heart and the plodding brain - <br>
surely between them there is some balance?<br><br>

Somewhere between memory and the future it rests.<br><br>
