<p><strong>Prototype 2: Factions - global multiplayer game</strong></p>
<p>Prototype Factions is a mobile- and web-based game whereby points are scored, status gained, content unlocked and prizes won by checking in to physical locations via Facebook Places and through digital locations.</p>
<p>Visual design for mobile Facebook app, as well as information architecture and wireframing.</p>
