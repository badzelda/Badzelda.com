<p> 
I was responsible for concept development, prototype UI design and coordinating in-house design and technical teams to deliver these apps. I also made sure that the final product delivered on the original concept, was true to the brand and ethos of the products, and worked for the target audience.</p> 
<p> 
All of the products were extensively user tested on kids, and I was responsible for setting up and conducting testing sessions, as well as documenting results.
