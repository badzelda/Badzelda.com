<p><strong>Lead service designer, NHS.uk / NHS App</strong></p>
<p>As a Lead designer for the Covid Testing programme, and subsequently NHS App, I helped to spin up and iterate services at a national scale, including messaging, access to care and new care delivery pathways.</p>
<p>Segmenting audiences, as-is experience / meta-mapping; synthesising user research; scenario mapping for policy support; blueprinting to-be experience and stakeholder mapping.</p>
<p>As Lead Service designer for the NHS App, I was responsible for a large design team, with the opportunity to have impact at scale. I worked with teams across the digital health landscape ensuring that designers worked with consistent patterns and approaches.</p>
<p>Design ops, training and mentoring: managing the NHS App design team. Visual, interaction and service design for the NHS App, including roadmap development and resource planning.</p>
