---I first started working for the London Review of Books in 2001, when I was approached by the Publisher, Nicholas Spice, to help them develop a stronger brand for their marketing materials.
<br><br>
We worked out a marketing brochure, and a 'look' for advertising, incorporating original photography (the music stand in the background, with a copy of the LRB on it) shot by myself. 
<br><br>
In addition to print design, I also worked with the LRB's technical team, architecting and designing their web site.
