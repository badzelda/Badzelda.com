<p><strong>Winnow - waste management at scale in commercial kitchens</strong></p>
<p>Winnow's clients range from mid to large scale enterprises, with users from kitchen staff to senior management. They need a very flexible way of showing data-intensive information that is scannable but allows them to dive into the detail.</p>
<p>Visual design of data and reporting created in Sketch, for the web admin interface.</p>
<p>The in-kitchen Android tablet app allows staff to categorise food waste, and view areas where wastage is prevalent. Visual and interaction design for the in-kitchen Android app.</p>
