<p><strong>Digital Development Editor, Tate Publishing</strong></p>
<p>I worked with Tate on design and development of the 'Noisy Neighbours' app — a story and activity app for kids. On opening, the child sees all the story's locations as coloured stripes; touching a stripe jumps the app to that location, where animations and audio invite the child into the narrative before panning on to the next.</p>
<p>This included remote research sessions using Lookback, plus mobile/app testing with TestFlight.</p>
<p><strong>Continuous improvement: Tate membership</strong></p>
<p>I project managed and provided UX deliverables for the rollout of new Members, Patrons and Donations forms on Tate's website, as well as launching a new members' area. This was a six-month project, working as an external contractor for Tate Digital and Tate Members.</p>
<p>Journey mapping and baselining analytics to understand the impact of design changes over time.</p>
