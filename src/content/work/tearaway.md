<p><strong>Creative technologist / researcher, Tearaway App</strong></p>
<p>Tearaway is a platform adventure video game developed for the PlayStation Vita, which was launched with a companion app. I recruited, and ran multiple rounds of usability research, working with developers to refine the concept and interface.</p>
<p>UX design. User research recruitment and session management. Documentation and developer collaboration.</p>
<p>Tabulating user experiences across multiple sessions helped developers understand problem areas in detail, allowing them to refine the user experience.</p>
